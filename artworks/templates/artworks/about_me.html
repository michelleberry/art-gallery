    About The Artist

    Michelle Berry was born in Ontario, Canada in 2001.
    She began teaching herself to draw portraits out of boredom in 2017. While learning to draw, she would observe photographs as
    references for her art. Since then, the face and portraiture has always been her main focus, however she has been working
    towards abandoning references in favour of a more personal and cartoonish style that she feels is more creative.

    In 2019, while studying Computer Science at the University of Guelph, Michelle initally hoped to complete a minor in studio arts.
    Unfortunately, the heavy time commitment to studio art classes discouraged her, as her compulsory courses were already taking
    a large chunk of her time. Even so, Michelle has continued to dedicate her spare time to producing drawings and sketches.
    Most recently, she has made a foray into producing digital artworks with the aid of a drawing tablet.

    In an ideal future, Michelle would blend her interests of studio art and computer science into a career.
    Recently she has programmed a small ice fishing game using processing that features her own original artwork as graphics.
